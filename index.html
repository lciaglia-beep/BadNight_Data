NODE 05

Satellite activation — Early talent ecosystem

Satellite Profile

Satellites are creators who orbit the project’s main author, joining the platform at different stages of development. They complete a verified registration process that allows for proper evaluation, and active membership is required to participate in the project’s collaboration ecosystem. This network may also include actors and recognized professionals from the audiovisual industry, whose visible support strengthens the project’s credibility, visibility, and commercial positioning.
